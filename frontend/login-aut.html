Terms & Conditions — Authorized Authorities

1. Authorized Access Only

Access to P.E.P.P.E.R’s authority panel is strictly limited to verified government agencies, law enforcement bodies, disaster management authorities, and approved partner organizations. Credentials are non-transferable.

2. Purpose-Bound Usage

The platform shall be used only for official purposes such as locating missing persons, disaster response, or lawful investigations. Any personal, political, or unauthorized use is strictly prohibited.

3. Human Verification Mandate

All AI-generated matches, signals, or similarity scores are assistive outputs only. Final identification, confirmation, and action must be performed by authorized human officials.

4. Data Confidentiality & Handling

All accessed data is confidential and classified. Authorities must ensure secure handling, storage, and usage of data in compliance with applicable data protection and privacy laws.

5. No Automated Decision-Making

P.E.P.P.E.R does not provide automated decisions. Any operational or legal decision taken based on system output lies entirely with the authorized authority.

9. Legal & Ethical Compliance

Authorities must operate within local, national, and international laws, including human rights, privacy, and data protection regulations.

10. System Integrity

Any attempt to tamper with system logs, bypass safeguards, extract unauthorized data, or reverse-engineer the platform will result in immediate access termination and potential legal action.
