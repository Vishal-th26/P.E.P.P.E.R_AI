How to Use PEPPER

Get Help with P.E.P.P.E.R

P.E.P.P.E.R is a secure, human-assisted platform designed to help locate missing individuals by responsibly connecting citizens with authorized authorities.

What is P.E.P.P.E.R?

P.E.P.P.E.R (Personal Embedded People Profiling & Enhanced Recognition) is an assistance system, not a decision-making authority. It uses AI-based facial recognition solely to support investigations under human supervision.

The system never confirms identities independently. All potential matches are reviewed, verified, and acted upon by authorized officials only.

How P.E.P.P.E.R Helps Citizens

  • Submit missing person reports in a secure and structured manner
  • Upload images or information without accessing any sensitive databases
  • Maintain privacy and anonymity where required
  • Enable faster communication with responsible authorities

Citizens can only submit information. They cannot view, retrieve, or modify any records.

How P.E.P.P.E.R Supports Authorities

  • AI-assisted face matching to reduce investigation time
  • Restricted access limited to verified organizations
  • Audit logs to ensure transparency and accountability
  • Human validation at every critical decision point

Ethical & Legal Commitment

P.E.P.P.E.R is built with a strict ethical framework. It does not automate legal decisions, does not replace human judgment, and does not permit unrestricted access to sensitive data.

Technology assists. Humans decide.
